Tuesday, April 30th

Voice Recordings

Structure

2 FP students, 2 HS students, 2 puppets

  • Introduce them to your character and to their dilemma (i.e. this is William and he's dealing with being misunderstood.)
  • Share different scenarios, and use the other character and other FP student to make the story:
    • What do you think they would do/think/feel if ...
    • FOLLOW UP QUESTIONS...
    • Have you ever X? What advice would you give?

How to Record

  • Keep mic/phone mic appx fist away from chin
  • Go to quit-ish place without echoes (e.g. use sweatshirt as cushion if need to)
